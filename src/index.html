Aura Traits

I designed Aura Traits, a personality prediction website, in Figma, focusing on a seamless and engaging user experience. The platform features a clean and modern UI, an interactive test flow with dynamic progress indicators, and a personalized results page that provides insightful personality analysis. The design is optimized for desktop ensuring accessibility and ease of use.

Coffee Haven

I designed Coffee Haven, a premium coffee e-commerce landing page, in Figma, focusing on a warm and inviting user experience. The design features a captivating hero section with a call-to-action, a visually appealing showcase of coffee blends, and an engaging subscription offer. The clean, modern layout ensures seamless navigation, making it easy for users to explore and shop for their favorite brews.

Bistro Delight

I designed Bistro Delight, a restaurant landing page, in Figma, focusing on an inviting and seamless user experience. The design features a stunning hero section showcasing signature dishes, an engaging "About Us" section, and a visually appealing menu with featured items. The layout also includes a reservation CTA for easy table bookings and highlights customer reviews, ensuring users feel welcome and encouraged to visit. The clean, responsive design makes navigation effortless, whether on desktop or mobile.
